Bihać Hip Hop Timeline

Late 1970s
Beytz D begins listening to Break Beat and Funk (Sugar Hill, Enjoy Records).
1985/1986
Electroshock Crew forms: DJ Vuki, MC Fresh Mix, Mister B, MCA.
1987
Beytz D moves to Zagreb for university and connects Bihać to the wider Hip Hop network.
1989
First major Bihać Hip Hop party held at Club Azra, Hotel Park.
Early 1990s
Screw Boys write internal hit "NIGHT KILLER" inspired by the movie "Colors." The group disbands due to the Yugoslav war.
